Founded

M/S A Q LEATHER was established in 1999, in the southern part of Kolkata by Mr. Md. Ahmed. Our Company is one of the leading companies of in-house manufacturer and exporter of leather goods that gives customized service. For more than two decades we have proved our mettle and manufactured an array of leather accessories like wallet, purse, credit card holder, file folder, etc. We practice ethical business and our products are unblemished in form. Constant research and use of updated machineries have made way for top of the line products. Our demand of leather is fulfilled from wet blue hide of cow, buffalo and goat. Sometimes we also purchase leather from Chinese and Italian Tanneries.

Mission

We enjoy an edge over our contemporaries, thanks to a team of highly specialized personnel like expert craftsmen, creative designers, leather connoisseurs, quality personnel, skilled labor and many more who make sure that international standards are met as an end result. The efficiency and hard work with which the products are designed and developed manifests the commitment with which they are worked upon.

Customer Satisfaction

We have a belief of treating our customer as a king and know that they are the sole reason behind our existence. This has led to our strong rooted establishment in the leather market and a long lasting relationship with our clients. Improvements and innovations in our workshop have gained solid ground in foreign lands like Israel, Austria, Germany, Poland, Italy, France, and Australia apart from our Indian customers. By keeping in touch with our customers we learn about their requirements and as per their needs we provide them the exact quality that they want. Timely delivery in sound condition is another reason behind our reputation. We deal in both individual and bulk orders. They regard suggestions by customers and come out with improvised versions of the products.

Achivements

A Q LEATHER, as an inhouse producer and exporter of leather items has a strong infrastructure and resource on which the business is based. By using state of the art equipment, which results in excellent produce, we have won hearts in India as well as across the seas.
